Governance Readiness Checklist

A light-weight framework to define the governance model in your software project.

A checklist?

The definition of Governance Rules is hard. We have created a checklist to help you to face common questions and issues you usually find when defining governance models.

The checklist was carefully created by a group of Open-Source developers and practitioners who worked together in the context of the Governance Readiness working group, an initiative of SustainOSS.

We identified three main dimensions that you should consider when defining your governance model. Elements in these dimensions have been meticulously selected and prioritized via a survey. For each dimension, we will show you five most important elements but you can always show the full list. You can find the full selection and prioritization process here.
